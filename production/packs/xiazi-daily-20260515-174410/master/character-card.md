## Allowed Variations

- Facial expression within the same Xiazi identity.
- Pose and hand gesture.
- Simple lifestyle props: book, suitcase, camera, map, laptop, notebook, checklist, coffee cup, calendar, phone, headset, lightbulb, small bag.
- Small scene cues related to reading, travel, work, rest, or daily greetings.
- Minor outfit additions such as cardigan, scarf, backpack, or travel bag, as long as they do not cover or replace the locked identity traits.
- Caption-specific action marks, sparkles, sweat drop, motion marks, or small hearts, all kept inside the final safe area.

## Forbidden Variations

- Changing Xiazi into an animal, mascot object, robot, or another species.
- Changing the age impression away from young woman.
- Changing the hair length, bangs structure, dark hair color identity, face shape, large round eyes, blush style, or warm chibi rendering without approval.
- Removing the approachable everyday personality and turning the pack into a distant fashion portrait set.
- Adding unrelated characters.
- Adding unapproved logos, trademarks, copyrighted IP, or personally sensitive content.
- Using photorealistic rendering.
- Using a dark, bitter, or complaint-heavy overtime theme as the main direction.
- Letting image generation invent, rewrite, or misspell production captions.
- Placing final sticker, cover, or icon content on a baked white, checkerboard, or hard square background.

## Caption Style

- Exact captions must come from `spec.json` or another explicit config.
- Prefer local text composition for final captions.
- Default style: white fill, thick dark brown outline, warm yellow or peach accent shadow.
- Placement should avoid covering eyes, mouth, hands, and the main expression.
